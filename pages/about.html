Innovation.gov is the home of the Better Government Movement--a crowdsourced, open source movement brought to you by the Technology Transformation Service at the General Services Administration and built by thousands of innovators across the federal government.

The Better Government Movement strives to create a more responsive and collaborative modern government, addressing the public’s needs directly. We define innovation as a new product, program, service, or process that creates value and changes the status quo of government services. To be impactful, innovations must also be scalable not merely one-off novelties. Federal government innovation means delivering a better government with and For the People.

We’ve built Innovation.gov to be the definitive source for and resources to help build public sector innovation. This site will help answer:

  • What do we mean by a better government?
  • Why should we innovate?
  • How you can bring innovative practices to your agency?
  • Who is innovating and where it is innovation happening?
  • What resources are available to learn and grow as an innovator?

Innovation.gov has the resources and networks needed to engage with innovative practices across the federal government. As you read through this site, we encourage you to:

  • Understand the principles of public sector innovation with the Playbook
  • Explore resources with our Toolkit
  • Attend and learn with our Community of Practice
  • Amplify and evangelize innovation with the Innovation Ambassadors
  • Read more about how people are innovating in the Stories section

Wherever you are in your journey, Innovation.gov is here to help.
