The U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) is committed to making all of our Internet content accessible to everyone. To ensure that we meet or exceed the requirements of the 1998 Amendment to Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, we continually review this site and modify web pages and documents to improve and enhance accessibility for people with disabilities.

If you have any difficulty with our site, please contact us at better-gov@gsa.gov.
