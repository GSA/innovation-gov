The Better Government Toolkit provides resources to build a better government through innovation.

Purpose

To learn modern ways to build a 21st century culture and workforce, improve government services delivery, solve complex problems, and work with innovators inside and outside of government.

Better Government Toolkit

  • Build a 21st Century Culture + Workforce

    Modern hiring practices, innovative acquisitions, and how create a culture of innovation within your agency.

  • Improve Government Services Delivery

    Modern, innovative methods such as human-centered design, Lean and Agile, and evidence-based approaches.

  • Solve Complex Societal + Governmental Problems

    How to identify and pursue grand challenges facing our nation, and creating a Community of Practice

  • Bring Innovators into Government

    Incentivizing innovation through prizes and challenges, citizen science and crowdsourcing, engaging startups and public-private partnerships.
