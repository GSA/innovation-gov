The Better Government Movement was officially launched with a series of human-centered design workshops in March 2017. Throughout 2017 the Community of Practice will collectively build a toolkit and create strategies to amplify and evangelize innovation across the federal government. Here is our mission:

We transform the way the people of government work together, guided by principles, with realignment of, or new investment in, policies, processes, and technology.

With a focus on efficiency, effectiveness, and accountability, we’ll create a more responsive and collaborative modern government, speaking directly toward the public’s needs and desires.

Innovation.gov and the Better Government Movement is a crowdsourced, open source movement brought to you by the General Services Administration, and built by hundreds of innovators across the Federal government.

We're from the government, and we're here to help.
